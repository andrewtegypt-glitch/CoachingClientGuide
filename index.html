16-Week Executive Coaching Roadmap

Week 1: Baseline & Alignment

Session 1 of 16
  • Define 16-week success metrics
  • Establish confidential boundaries
  • Review career trajectory and 15-year leadership journey
Between sessions: Complete the 'Values & Vision' audit.

Week 2: The Inner Game of Leadership

Session 2 of 16
  • Initial 360-degree feedback review
  • Identifying blind spots in executive presence
  • Strengths-based leadership mapping
Between sessions: Request feedback from 3 direct reports and 2 peers.

Week 3: ADHD & The Executive Brain

Session 3 of 16
  • Understanding neurodiversity in high-stakes environments
  • Mapping personal 'Cognitive Load' patterns
  • Identifying executive function strengths/gaps
Between sessions: Keep a 3-day 'Energy & Focus' log.

Week 4: Precision Systems Design

Session 4 of 16
  • Custom 'external brain' systems for ADHD
  • Optimising calendar and task architecture
  • The 'One-Touch' rule for communication
Between sessions: Implement the agreed digital workspace structure.

Week 5: Strategic Decision Making

Session 5 of 16
  • Overcoming analysis paralysis and impulsivity
  • Building custom decision-making frameworks
  • Stakeholder impact analysis
Between sessions: Apply the 'Decision Matrix' to one major current choice.

Week 6: High-Stakes Communication

Session 6 of 16
  • Managing board and investor expectations
  • The art of the 'Executive Brief'
  • Active listening as a strategic tool
Between sessions: Record one meeting (with permission) for self-audit.

Week 7: Emotional Intelligence (EQ)

Session 7 of 16
  • Self-regulation in high-pressure moments
  • Empathy as a leadership lever
  • Conflict resolution frameworks
Between sessions: Practice 'The Pause' in one challenging interaction.

Week 8: Mid-Engagement Reflection

Session 8 of 16
  • Progress audit against Week 1 metrics
  • Course correction and focus sharpening
  • Refining the ADHD systems established
Between sessions: Write a short 'Current State' memo to yourself.

Week 9: Team Performance & Culture

Session 9 of 16
  • Building psychological safety in your team
  • Delegation as a growth mechanism
  • Performance management vs. performance coaching
Between sessions: Hold one developmental 1:1 using the coaching model.

Week 10: Influence & Stakeholder Power

Session 10 of 16
  • Mapping the political landscape of the organisation
  • Ethical influence and alliance building
  • Navigating corporate silos
Between sessions: Map your 'Circle of Influence' for a key project.

Week 11: Strategic Thinking & Innovation

Session 11 of 16
  • Moving from 'Operator' to 'Strategist'
  • Creating space for deep work and visioning
  • Managing the innovation pipeline
Between sessions: Schedule 2 hours of 'Thinking Time' with zero devices.

Week 12: Resilience & Burnout Prevention

Session 12 of 16
  • Building personal support infrastructure
  • Design sustainable rhythms: energy management
  • Identify burnout warning signals
Between sessions: Identify 3 people outside the org for honest support.

Week 13: Financial Acumen for Leaders

Session 13 of 16
  • Interpreting P&L and Balance Sheets for strategy
  • Capital allocation and ROI thinking
  • Risk assessment and mitigation
Between sessions: Review your current budget through a 'Growth' lens.

Week 14: Succession & Legacy

Session 14 of 16
  • Define the leader you want to be remembered as
  • Succession planning and lifting others
  • Building mentoring into regular practice
Between sessions: Write a 1-page 'Leadership Legacy Statement'.

Week 15: Full Engagement Review

Session 15 of 16
  • Review all 16-week goals against outcomes
  • Acknowledge growth and remaining edges
  • Collect informal re-360 feedback
Between sessions: Ask the Session 2 respondents for updated input.

Week 16: Forward Strategy & Renewal

Session 16 of 16
  • Build a 6-month post-engagement plan
  • Design a self-coaching practice
  • Agree follow-on structure if applicable
Post-engagement: Share outcomes with your sponsor. Schedule 6-month check-in.
