4 Sessions Over 4 Weeks

Momentum Program

Best for professionals who want structure, accountability, and a meaningful shift in one focused area over a month.

WeekSessionFocus

Session-by-Session Content

Session 1: Goal Setting and Baseline

  • What does success look like in 4 weeks?
  • Where are you now vs where you want to be?
  • What are the top 3 priorities?
  • Set the coaching agreement and working style

Session 2: Strategy and Blockers

  • What actions did you take this week?
  • Explore the biggest blocker in depth
  • Build a simple action plan for weeks 3 and 4
  • Identify one habit or system to introduce
